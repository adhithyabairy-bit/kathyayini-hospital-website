Our Services

Comprehensive Eye Care
Under One Roof

From routine eye exams to advanced surgical procedures, we offer a full spectrum of ophthalmic services.

🔭

Cataract Surgery

Advanced cataract care including Phacoemulsification (Laser Cataract Surgery) and Micro Incision Cataract Surgery (MICS) with premium intraocular lens options — Multifocal, Toric, and EDOF IOLs.

Learn More →
⚡

Nd-YAG Laser

Quick, painless laser procedure to clear posterior capsule clouding (PCO) that can develop after cataract surgery, restoring sharp and bright vision immediately.

Learn More →
👁️

Contact Lens Services

Professional contact lens evaluation, fitting, and expert guidance to ensure comfort, clarity, and safety for long-lasting visual performance.

Learn More →
👶

Paediatric Eye Care

Dedicated child eye care promoting healthy vision through early detection, accurate treatment, and gentle examinations designed specifically for young patients.

Learn More →
🩺

General Eye Examination

Comprehensive eye exams including visual acuity testing, refraction, intraocular pressure measurement, and full anterior and posterior segment evaluation.

Learn More →
💊

Glaucoma Care

Early detection, monitoring, and management of glaucoma using advanced diagnostic equipment and personalized treatment protocols to protect your sight.
